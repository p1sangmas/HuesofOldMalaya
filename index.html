About the Project

Hues of Old Malaya is a groundbreaking initiative that merges the precision of artificial intelligence with the beauty of historical preservation. Our advanced neural networks are trained to understand the essence of Malaysia's cultural heritage, recoloring black-and-white photographs with hues that echo the vibrancy of the past.

From bustling streets of colonial Malaya to serene kampung landscapes, our AI ensures every detail is revived with authenticity and care. This is more than recolorization—it's a journey through time, where technology meets tradition to rekindle the soul of history.

Technology Behind Hues of Old Malaya

Deep Learning

At the heart of Hues of Old Malaya lies a custom-built GAN (Generative Adversarial Network) architecture, designed to analyze and predict colors with remarkable accuracy. Our AI doesn’t just add color—it interprets the context, ensuring every shade reflects the historical and cultural essence of Malaysia.

Computer Vision

With cutting-edge computer vision techniques, we preserve intricate details, from the delicate patterns of batik to the vibrant tones of traditional attire. Optimized for speed and precision, our technology delivers breathtaking results in moments, making history accessible to all.
